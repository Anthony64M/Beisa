Places to Visit in Laikipia

This is a list of the top 10 best places to visit in Laikipia county . Laikipia is a cosmopolitan county located on the equator, in the former Rift Valley Province. It is one of the top tourist destinations in Kenya. It is endowed with natural resources and scenic attraction sites. The county is headed by Governor Joshua Irungu .

In no particular order, here is a list of the 10 best places to visit in Laikipia county.

1. Ol Pejeta Conservancy

It is the largest black rhino sanctuary in East Africa and home to two of the world's last remaining northern white rhino. It is also home to over 300 bird species and other wild animals such as lions, buffalos, elephants, zebras, leopards, impalas, gazelles, vervet monkeys and cheetahs. It is a perfect destination for game viewing, camping, bush walks and lion tracking.

2. Ol Jogi Wildlife Conservancy

It is Kenya's second largest rhino sanctuary. It is also home to endangered wild species such as the reticulated giraffe, Grevy's zebra, elephants, lions, African wild dog, cheetah, greater kudu and Laikipia hartebeest. The Conservancy also operates as a working cattle ranch and features a state of the art veterinary clinic that serves the whole of Laikipia region.

3. Thomson's Falls

It is a scenic 74 meters waterfall situated about 3 kilometres from Nyahururu town. Upstream from the falls is one of the highest hippo pools in Kenya. You can visit Nyahururu town to have a glimpse of the spectacular waterfall which plunges down several meters into a fearful gorge.

4. Loisaba Conservancy

It is a 57,000 acre wildlife conservancy, established to protect and enhance critical wildlife diversity and habitat. It is a haven for over 200 species of birds, elephants, buffalos, Grevy's zebra, giraffes, lions, leopards and cheetahs. It is a perfect destination for game drives, bush walks, horse riding, camel riding, mountain biking and bird watching.

5. Mugie Conservancy

It is a private wildlife conservancy located in Laikipia. It is a home to over 100 bird species, animals such as buffalos, elephants, Grevy's zebra, reticulated giraffe, Jackson's hartebeest and storks. It also offers exquisite accommodation in tented camps. It is a nice place for game viewing activities, bush walks and bird watching.

6. Nanyuki Spinners and Weavers

Located at the Nanyuki Spinners and Weavers Factory, it is the best place to buy hand woven carpets, rugs, table mats, cloths and many other weaving products. They offer quality and beautiful hand woven cloths at affordable prices. You can also tour the factory and see how the weaving is done.

7. Nyahururu Antique and Crafts Village

Located in Nyahururu town, it is one of the best places to buy Kenyan crafts and antiques. You will find a variety of art pieces, wood carvings, Maasai beadwork, textiles, and traditional artifacts. It's a great place to shop for souvenirs and gifts.

8. Laikipia Maasai Beadwork Cooperative

It is a cooperative project that aims at providing a sustainable source of income to the local Maasai women. They make beautiful beaded jewelry and sell them to tourists. You can visit their workshops and see how these beautiful beads are made. Buying their products is a great way to support local women's empowerment.

9. Ngare Ndare Forest

It is a lush indigenous forest at the foothills of Mt. Kenya. It features a canopy walkway that is 40ft high and 450m long, offering a unique view of the forest and its wildlife. You can also swim in the clear blue pools and hike to the beautiful waterfalls in the forest.

10. Nyandarua Range

It is a mountain range in central Kenya, located in Laikipia County. It offers excellent opportunities for mountain climbing, hiking, and trekking. The range provides breathtaking views of the surrounding landscapes and is a paradise for adventure seekers.

These are just a few of the amazing places to visit in Laikipia County. The county offers a wide range of activities for nature lovers, wildlife enthusiasts, and adventure seekers. Whether you want to go on a game drive, hike in the mountains, or explore local crafts, Laikipia has something to offer everyone.

Plan your visit to Laikipia and explore the beauty of this diverse and vibrant county in Kenya.
