Places to Visit in Laikipia

A wildlife conservancy located on the slopes of Mount Kenya, offering a chance to see a variety of animals in their natural habitat.

Ol Pejeta Conservancy

A wildlife conservancy that is home to the largest black rhino sanctuary in East Africa, as well as other endangered species.

Lewa Wildlife Conservancy

A wildlife conservancy that is home to a variety of animals, including the endangered Grevy's zebra and black rhino. Visitors can go on game drives, guided walks, and horseback safaris.

Laikipia Plateau

A vast plateau that is home to a variety of wildlife, including elephants, lions, and leopards. Visitors can go on game drives, guided walks, and birdwatching tours.
